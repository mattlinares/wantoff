Sharing stuff in the neighbourhood — it actually works

28 June 2026 · Matt

I've been banging on about local exchange for years. The usual problem is that nobody shows up. You post something on a community board, it sits there, nothing happens. Last month I started using something called Wantoff which is trying to fix that by tying listings to your actual trust network in Circles.

Early days, but there's already a decent little cluster of people at our co-working space using it. Someone's lending a drill, someone else is offering Spanish lessons. It's not magic but it's working better than I expected.

You can see my profile below — I've got a hot desk available and I'm running Python sessions most weeks. If you're in the Easton area and want something, just request it through the app.

↑ live from Wantoff

The reputation side of things is interesting too — it's building on Circles, so your score partly reflects who trusts you in the network, not just reviews. Still rough but the direction feels right.

More on this once I've had a few more exchanges through it.
